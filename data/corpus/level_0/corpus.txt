1. Simple Sentences
The cat sat on the mat.
I like to eat apples.
She is reading a book.
They went to the park.
He plays soccer every day.
We are happy to see you.
The sun is shining brightly.
Birds fly in the sky.
My favorite color is blue.
The dog barks loudly.
2. Common Conversational Phrases
How are you today?
What is your name?
Where are you from?
Can you help me, please?
Thank you very much!
I don’t understand.
What time is it?
Do you like pizza?
Let’s go for a walk.
Have a nice day!
3. Basic Questions and Answers
Q: What is your favorite food?
A: My favorite food is pasta.
Q: Where do you live?
A: I live in a small town.
Q: How old are you?
A: I am ten years old.
Q: What do you do for fun?
A: I like to play video games.
Q: Do you have any pets?
A: Yes, I have a dog.
4. Short Stories and Narratives
Once upon a time, there was a little rabbit. It loved to hop in the forest.
Tom woke up early. He ate breakfast and went to school.
The sun was setting, and the sky turned orange. It was a beautiful evening.
A boy found a shiny coin on the ground. He picked it up and smiled.
The rain stopped, and a rainbow appeared. Everyone looked up in awe.
5. Descriptive Sentences
The sky is blue and clear.
The flowers are red and yellow.
The house is big and white.
The cake smells sweet and delicious.
The water is cold and refreshing.
6. Lists and Enumerations
I need to buy milk, bread, and eggs.
My favorite animals are dogs, cats, and birds.
The days of the week are Monday, Tuesday, Wednesday, Thursday, Friday, Saturday, and Sunday.
The colors of the rainbow are red, orange, yellow, green, blue, indigo, and violet.
